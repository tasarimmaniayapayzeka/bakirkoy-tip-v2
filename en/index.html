Your Journey

Four clear steps, one point of contact

From your first message to your follow-up at home, a dedicated patient coordinator stays with you at every step.

01

Share your records

Send your medical reports and questions via WhatsApp or the form below. Our physicians review them before you travel.

02

Receive your plan

Within 2 business hours you receive a personal treatment plan, a transparent cost estimate and available dates.

03

Arrive and be treated

Airport pickup, partner hotel nearby, and an interpreter at every consultation. Tests and results usually in one visit.

04

Fly home, stay connected

You leave with a full written report in English. Your coordinator arranges remote follow-up with your physician.

Popular Programs

Designed for visitors on a schedule

Outpatient programs that fit into a short stay in Istanbul — no hospitalization required.

01

Comprehensive Check-up

Full-body screening in a single morning: physician consultations, laboratory panel, ECG, ultrasound and a written report with same-day review. A convenient option for visitors combining a family trip with an annual check-up.

Women's Health Program
